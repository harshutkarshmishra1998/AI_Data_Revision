Tea is more than just a morning beverage; for many, tea is a daily ritual. People love tea because tea provides a quick burst of energy, but the love for tea goes deeper than just caffeine. The aroma of freshly brewed tea can instantly brighten a gloomy day, making tea the ultimate comfort drink. Whether you prefer iced tea, hot tea, or a complex espresso blend, tea remains a universally cherished beverage across the globe.
